Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings

Steps to make

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar in a bowl. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Knead the dough until soft and smooth.
  3. Cover and let the dough rise for about 1 hour.
