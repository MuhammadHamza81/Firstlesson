Lasagna Recipe

this is lasagna recipe the item which is very famous in italy

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps to make it

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the ground beef and mix with sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
